🐸 Frog Crossing

Cross the road without getting hit!

How to Play

  • 🎯 Reach the green zone at the top to score
  • ❤️ You start with 3 lives — avoid the cars!
  • ⭐ Cross 5 times to reach the next level
  • 🚀 Every level the cars get faster
  • ➕ You earn +1 life on every level up

Use Arrow Keys or the D-pad below to move
